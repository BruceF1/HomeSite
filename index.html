ABOUT
TWO WAVES
My journey into the world of design and tech has been quite an adventure...

I began my career studying 3D animation, which led to a role as a 3D designer in the point-of-sale industry. Along the way, I explored product and architectural visualization in my spare time, which greatly expanded my creative skill set.

Inspired to take on new challenges, I started a design company focused on coworking spaces. This venture introduced me to web design and development, adding a valuable new dimension to my expertise.

Later, I worked on creating rich content applications in Flash before returning to point-of-sale design. However, the COVID-19 pandemic brought significant changes to my career. After being retrenched, I transitioned to software development by learning Ruby on Rails on the job at Puzzl, a startup I helped launch.

This experience sparked my passion for software development and eventually led me to the cybersecurity industry, where I now work with ASP.NET on a web application called Encore. I genuinely enjoy what I do, but I am always eager for new challenges and opportunities to grow.

Currently, I am seeking additional work and experience in cybersecurity or perhaps the next big step in my career. Thank you for taking the time to learn about my journey—I’m excited about the future and look forward to connecting with others who share my passion for technology and innovation.
